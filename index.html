Who was Turing?
Turing played a large role in the deciphering of German code during WWII
"Turing's paper, 'On Computable Numbers, with an Application to the Entscheidungsproblem'...he presented the notion of a universal machine, capable of computing anything." (biography.com)
Concept of modern computers is based off this paper.
In his other paper, 'Computing machinery and intelligence,' Turing created the Turing Test(biography.com)
The Turing Test is used to determine whether or not a computer can think.
"A remote human interrogator, within a fixed time frame, must distinguish between a computer and a human subject based on their replies to various questions posed by the interrogator."(Turing test)
The work of Turing is acknowledged as the basis of research in Artificial Intelligence (AI)
